We invite you to complete a collaborative building task on Minecraft playing against an artificial intelligent Agent.

You'll have to open minecraft and connect to a particular server. Once there, you will instroduce the code given in this HIT and will be teleported to a world where the task till begin. You will see a target structure composed of blocks, and the another player, called the Builder.

The goal of this HIT is to give short instructions to the Builder explaining what blocks to place or remove to progress from the current structure towards the target structure you can see behind the wall.

Make sure to leave this window open as you complete the survey. When you are finished, you will return to this page to paste the completioncode into the box.
